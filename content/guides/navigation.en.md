## Gate Opening & Official Opening Ceremony

Entry and initial setup at the festival:

*   **Gate Opening:** The festival gates open on Friday, July 24, at 12:00 PM. We recommend arriving on this day to pick a good spot and set up camp comfortably before things get busy.
*   **Official Opening Ceremony:** Takes place on Monday, July 27, at 20:00 on the main stage (Ozora Stage). This is a major community gathering where attendees come together to mark the start of the music program on the main stage.

## Getting Around the Festival Grounds

The festival grounds in Dádpuszta are vast, hilly, and can be tricky to navigate, especially after dark:

*   **Pin Your Camp Digitally:** Drop a pin for your tent's location in a navigation app (such as Google Maps or this app's map).
*   **Navigate by Landmarks:** Use fixed, prominent landmarks lit up at night — giant sculptures, distinctive hills, or art installations.
*   **Plan Ahead:** Agree on a clear, fixed meeting point with your friends (for example, "by the World Tree statue at 14:00") in case you lose cell signal or your phone battery dies.

## Key Art Installations & Landmarks

Unique wooden structures and sculptures are scattered across the grounds, serving as prominent meeting points and landmarks:

*   **Adam and Eve Statue (Ádám és Éva):** An impressive wooden sculpture located in the center of the garden area.
*   **World Tree (Világfa):** An iconic, massive wooden structure standing near the main stage — one of the festival's best-known and most popular meeting points.
*   **Butterfly Lookout (Pillangó):** A wooden observation structure shaped like a butterfly, overlooking the festival grounds from above. Worth visiting at sunrise and sunset.
*   **Herb Garden:** A quiet, well-tended community garden dedicated to medicinal herbs and fragrant local plants.

## Sustainability & the Recycling Center

Festival management is committed to sustainability and protecting the natural environment:

*   **Trash Bags at Entry:** You'll receive dedicated trash bags when you enter the festival gates. Please collect your personal waste and dispose of it at the collection points around the site.
*   **Recycling Center:** A dedicated center for sorting and recycling cans and bottles operates on-site. Help keep the festival clean and green!

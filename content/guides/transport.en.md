## Flights to Budapest & Initial Prep

Budapest's Ferenc Liszt International Airport is the main entry point for most attendees arriving from outside Hungary:

*   **Choosing an Airline:** Given frequent changes to international schedules, we strongly recommend prioritizing Israeli carriers (such as El Al, Israir, or Arkia), which tend to offer more stable flights to and from Israel.
*   **Arriving Early:** We recommend landing in Budapest at least two days before the festival gates open. This gives you time to shop for camping gear and food at a relaxed pace before heading to the festival.

## Getting from Budapest to Simontornya by Train

Traveling by train is a convenient, efficient, and budget-friendly way to reach the festival:

1.  Head to **Budapest Déli** train station.
2.  Board a train toward **Simontornya** (the ride takes about 1.5–2 hours).
3.  **Buy Tickets in Advance:** We strongly recommend downloading the official Hungarian rail app (**MÁV**) and buying your tickets ahead of time, since trains get very crowded during festival days.

## Mandatory Vehicle Registration

Per the festival management's guidelines, bringing any vehicle onto the festival grounds requires advance registration on the website:

*   **Which Vehicles Need Registration:** Every motorized, license-plated vehicle (car, campervan/RV, motorcycle, or trailer) must be registered in advance before arrival.
*   **Vehicle Registration Link:** [vehicle.dadpuszta.hu](https://vehicle.dadpuszta.hu/)
*   **Details Required for Registration:** Vehicle type, model, color, license plate number, and estimated arrival date.
*   **Rental Cars:** If you're renting a car in Budapest, register it as soon as you receive the vehicle and its details from the rental company. Don't wait until you reach the festival gates — doing so can cause significant entry delays.

## Traveling by Private Car or Taxi

The drive from Budapest to the festival site in Dádpuszta takes about two hours under normal road conditions:

*   **Traffic on Opening Day:** Expect heavy traffic near the entrance on gate-opening day (July 24). Confirm with your taxi or driver that they're prepared to wait in line and won't drop you off far from the main gate.
*   **Taxi Access:** Taxis are not allowed inside the festival grounds. They will drop you off at the main gate only.
*   **Entry Process with a Private Car:** If you arrive in your own car, you must first park in the main external parking lot, walk to the box office to get your personal entry wristband, and only then return to your car to drive into the camping area.

## Shuttles from the Train Station to the Festival

Right outside the train station in the town of Simontornya:

*   **Dedicated Shuttles:** Dedicated festival buses and minibuses (Ozorashuttle) are waiting there.
*   **Frequency & Availability:** Shuttles run frequently around the clock, taking attendees directly to the festival gates in about 10–15 minutes.
*   **Payment:** Paid in cash on-site (in euros or Hungarian forints).
*   **Direct Shuttles from the Airport:** For a direct shuttle service from Budapest airport straight to the festival, you can book seats in advance on the official site [ozorashuttle.com](https://www.ozorashuttle.com).

## Main Music Stages

The festival grounds host several music stages offering a variety of styles and sound experiences:

*   **Ozora Stage:** The main stage and beating heart of the festival. Features a powerful sound system and a huge, colorful, elaborately designed canopy. This stage hosts the official opening ceremony and features psytrance, goa, and progressive music from top artists worldwide.
*   **The Dome:** An impressive architectural structure dedicated to alternative music, downtempo, ambient, and deep techno. Known for high-quality sound and 360-degree projection mapping across the ceiling. An ideal space to rest and unwind.
*   **Pumpui:** The main stage for techno and house fans. Runs almost continuously throughout the festival and is located relatively close to the camping areas.
*   **Dragon Nest:** A unique stage for live performances blending folk, reggae, world music, and acoustic projects built around traditional instruments.
*   **Ambyss:** Located on the lakeshore, entirely dedicated to ambient, chillout, and slow trance music.
*   **Tek Zero:** A musical space dedicated to nostalgic trance styles from the early 2000s.

## Workshops & Spiritual Spaces

The festival offers a rich variety of enrichment activities, talks, and workshops throughout the day:

*   **Chambok House:** Hosts talks on philosophy, science, psychology, sustainability, and complementary medicine.
*   **Compass:** A center for interactive workshops, discussion circles, and community dialogue.
*   **Healion:** A space dedicated to body and mind wellness, offering yoga, meditation, qigong, and a variety of alternative treatments led by certified practitioners.

## Art & Creative Spaces

Spaces for creative, artistic, and cultural expression across the festival grounds:

*   **Artibarn:** An open craft workshop where attendees can create on their own. Offers pottery, woodworking, clay sculpting, painting, and jewelry-making workshops.
*   **Circus:** Hosts acrobatics, street theater, and stunning fire-juggling performances in the evening, alongside hands-on juggling workshops during the day.
*   **Mirador Gallery:** A stunning exhibition space showcasing visual and psychedelic artwork from artists around the world.

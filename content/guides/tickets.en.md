## Ticket Purchase Steps

Official, valid festival tickets are purchased securely through the official website at [ticket.ozorafestival.eu](https://ticket.ozorafestival.eu):

1.  **Initial Registration:** Create an account with your full name and email address, and accept the festival's terms, which include Ozora's 13 core principles.
2.  **Identity Verification (Ozora ID):** You'll receive an automatic email with a link to verify your details ("Check my Ozora ID"). Enter your passport details exactly as they appear on the document. Make sure your name and personal details match your ID exactly!
3.  **Payment:** Ticket payment is made in euros (EUR) through the secure Simple Pay system.

## Ticket Check & Entry Guidelines (Wristband Info)

To avoid delays or being denied entry at the festival gates, follow these guidelines ahead of time:

*   **Check Your Ticket:** We recommend verifying your ticket in advance via the official link: [ticket.ozorafestival.eu/check](https://ticket.ozorafestival.eu/check).
    Make sure:
    *   The name on the ticket matches your passport or ID exactly ✅
    *   Your date of birth is entered correctly ✅
    *   Your citizenship and nationality details are accurate ✅
    *   The attached photo is a clear, valid selfie-style portrait (avoid photographing your ID document itself, sunglasses, or hats covering your face) ✅
*   **Personalized Tickets:** Festival tickets are personalized and cannot be transferred without an official process. If the details on the ticket don't match the physical ID shown at the gate, **entry will be denied.**
*   **Show a Physical, Original ID:** You must present an original, physical ID document (a valid passport or national ID card).
    *   **Note:** A photo of your passport or a picture of the document on your phone **is not accepted as valid ID at the festival gates under any circumstances.**

## Payment Methods & Balance Settlement

The festival website offers two ways to pay for your ticket:

*   **Partial Payment:** Pay a €120 deposit by credit card on the website, then settle the remaining balance in cash at the festival box office on arrival.
    *   **Time-Saving Tip (Settle Online):** To speed up entry and avoid long waits, we recommend settling your remaining balance online *before* arriving at the festival. Paying in advance lets you go straight to the fast-track "Approved" ticket desks (Desks A). You can complete the payment here: [Settle Your Ticket Balance](https://ticket.ozorafestival.eu/check/?action=ticket-check-my-form).
    *   **Important Notes for Paying the Balance On-Site (if not settled in advance):**
        *   **Same Currency:** The balance must be paid on-site in the same currency as your initial online payment (euros only).
        *   **Cash Only:** The festival box office accepts **cash only** (credit cards are not accepted at the gates, and change may not be available in small denominations).
        *   **Bring the Exact Amount:** We recommend bringing the exact balance in cash to speed up the process.
*   **Full Payment:** Pay the full ticket price upfront on the website by credit card, allowing direct entry with no further payment steps at the gates.

*Note:* If your credit card issuer blocks the payment (since the vendor is Hungarian), contact your card issuer to approve an international purchase. Your ticket is only valid and confirmed once you receive the official PDF by email.

## Reselling & Transferring Tickets

Festival tickets cannot be cancelled or refunded. However, you can officially and securely transfer a ticket to another participant:

1.  **Fill Out the Official Ticket Transfer Form** on the festival website.
2.  The seller and buyer will each receive an email to confirm the transfer.
3.  Once both sides confirm, the original ticket is cancelled and a new one is issued in the buyer's name.

*Note:* Per the festival's terms, the buyer will be required to pay the difference between the original purchase price and the current ticket price listed on the website at the time of transfer.

## Registering Children & Pets

*   **Registering Children Under 14:** Entry for children under 14 is free. However, you must complete the official Kid Registration Form on the festival website in advance.
*   **Registering Pets:** Dogs are allowed on-site with advance registration on the festival website. You'll need to present a valid vaccination booklet (including a current rabies vaccination) and proof of microchipping. Dogs must be kept on a leash throughout the festival grounds.
